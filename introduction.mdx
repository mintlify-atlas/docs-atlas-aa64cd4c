## Why Mezo?

Mezo eliminates middlemen between you and your wealth:

- **Fixed Rates**: Borrow at rates starting from 1% that stay fixed for the entire life of the loan
- **Complete Autonomy**: No minimum payments, no set schedules, no early repayment penalties
- **Self-Service**: Manage everything yourself, 24/7, without permission
- **BTC as Gas**: All transactions paid in Bitcoin, keeping the experience pure Bitcoin
- **Verifiable Reserves**: Built on tBTC with 24k+ BTC bridged since 2020, reserves verifiable anytime

## Before You Begin

To use Mezo, you'll need:

1. A compatible wallet (MetaMask, Brave Wallet, TrustWallet, or a supported BTC wallet)
2. Bitcoin or supported ERC-20 tokens to deposit
3. Some BTC or tBTC for gas fees on the Mezo network

<Warning>
All transactions on Mezo Mainnet are paid using native BTC. Before using the network, fund your account with BTC or tBTC to ensure you have gas to pay for transactions.
</Warning>
