## Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage

- Use the provided logo files without modification
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" in Mezo
- Don't stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- Don't change the logo colors

### Color Palette

<Note>
**Primary Brand Color:** #FF004D (Mezo Red)

Use this color for primary brand elements, CTAs, and emphasis.
</Note>

### Typography

Mezo uses the **Riforma** typeface for headings and the system font stack for body text.

## Usage Rights

These brand assets are provided for:
- Partners and integrations featuring Mezo
- Developers building on Mezo
- Community content and education
- Press and media coverage

<Warning>
Do not use Mezo brand assets in ways that could:
- Imply official endorsement without permission
- Mislead users about affiliation with Mezo
- Violate trademark or intellectual property rights
</Warning>
